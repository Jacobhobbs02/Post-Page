

Project: Post-Page

-Like Twitter and Instagram this is a social media platform where you can make posts. 

-Index Page: Normal About Us type Page. Information about the platform.

-Source Page: this is a page with sources to the administration of Post Page.

-Login/Registration: Normal Login Page (includes validation)

-Dashboard: Shows all Posts. Your post of one side. Other post on the other. (All YOUR posts can be edited if you're logged in)

-Add Page: This is where you make your post and it will go to the Dashboard for everyone to see.

-Friends Page: This will show all your friends (full name) or they can be deleted.

-Discover Page: This will show all users who have an account so they can be added as a friend.
